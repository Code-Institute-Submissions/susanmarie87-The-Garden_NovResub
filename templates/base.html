On A Mission To Change Nutrition

Get to know our purpose

  • 2019-2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    It all began when Gabe, a twelve-year-old with special needs decided he had had enough with the nasty cafeteria food being offered

  • September 2020

    A Dream is Born

    Gabe's Mom and sister began working on creating a garden idea where children would become apart of their nutrition process by helping to grow the food and create the recipes being served for their meals.

  • December 2015

    Transition From Dream to Reality

    We are now in the final stages of bringning this dream to reality and need all of the community support we can get.

  • July 2021

    Phase Two Expansion

    Join us as we turn this dream into reality. There are a variety of ways that you can contibute. Sign Up to learn more!

Gabe's Garden

On a mission to change nutrition.

Fed up with the school cafeteria food, Gabriel Lee decided he wanted to change that. Together with his Mom and sister they are going to create a life-changing system where kids get to grow their food, calculate how much they will need, and create the recipes used for their harvests, bringing farm-to-table to reality for schools.
