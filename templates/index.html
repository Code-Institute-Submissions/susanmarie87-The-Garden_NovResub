Who We Are

In America, we have some of the world's worst food. The food supply is mostly processed, full of poison, and if it is not fake, processed, and full of poison, it is saturated in chemicals that cause cancer as a form of pest control. As a result, our nation is seeing some of the worst health outcomes in children. Over 40% of America's children suffer from chronic illness and cancer is a leading cause of death. We are here to change that.

Read Our Story

Our Mission

We would like to start the trend of school's growing a large percentage of their own organic food using natural pest control and regenerative farming. Through these simple methods, we can instill the value of knowing where your food comes from and what it means to grow your own food. We hope that these values will help to reduce the rates of chronic illness.

Dreamers

Creators

What better way to creat is there then by growing your own food? We will create healthy food, healthy kids, and a healthy planet from our sustainable farming practices.

Earth Healers

Our sustainable farming practices will mend the soil, reduce carbon emissions, and reeduce waste. All of these things are greatly needed for a healthy future for ourselves and the planet.
