Who We Are

Gabe's Garden was created when 12-year-old Gabe decided he was fed up with the horrible cafeteria food being served. America is home to some of the world's most unhealthy food. Our food supply is mostly processed, full of unhealthy genetically modifed organisms, and is often referred to as frankenfood. Our farms are saturated in chemicals that cause cancer as a form of pest control. As a result, our nation is seeing some of the worst health outcomes in children and adults. Over 40% of America's children suffer from chronic illness and cancer is a leading cause of death.
We are here to change that

Our Mission

Together with the help of his family and friends, Gabe's Garden will embark on a mission to change nutrition. The goal is to get as many schools as possible to begin growing their own food at the schools. The children will be apart of the entire process from seed to table. Through Regenerative farming and natural pest control we can reduce our carbon footprint, improve soil quality, and reduce the unnecessary use of toxic chemicals. Want to join us?
