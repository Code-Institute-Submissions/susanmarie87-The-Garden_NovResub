Who We Are

Fed up with the lack of nutritious food in school cafeterias, 12-year-old Gabe decided to try and change the way we eat at school. Gabe is teaming up with his community and school districts to implement an a farm=-to-table system that will change everything.
We are here to change that

Our Mission

Gabe's Garden is built on the dream of bringing gardens that provide the nutrition for kids into schools. Gabe's Garden will embark on a mission to change nutrition. The goal is to get as many schools as possible to begin growing their own food at the schools. The children will be apart of the entire process from seed to table. Through Regenerative farming and natural pest control we can reduce our carbon footprint, improve soil quality, and reduce the unnecessary use of toxic chemicals. Want to join us?
