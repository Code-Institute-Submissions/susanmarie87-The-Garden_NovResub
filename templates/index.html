Who We Are

America is home to some of the world's most unhealthy food. Our food supply is mostly processed, full of unhealthy genetically modifed organisms, and is often referred to as frankenfood. Our farms are saturated in chemicals that cause cancer as a form of pest control. As a result, our nation is seeing some of the worst health outcomes in children and adults. Over 40% of America's children suffer from chronic illness and cancer is a leading cause of death.
We are here to change that.

Our Mission

The dream we want to create is to see schools implement their own farms to grow the majority of their own food with natural methods. This seems monumental task but with the help of community and the kids, we should be able to accomplish this giant but worthwhile, dream.

Dreamers

We are tiny but mighty. Through hard work and dedication, we will turn our dreams into a reality.

Creators

What better way to creat is there then by growing your own food? We will create healthy food, healthy kids, and a healthy planet from our sustainable farming practices.

World Changers

Our ideals and methods will surely put kids and schools on course to not only change nutrition, but ultimately change the world.
