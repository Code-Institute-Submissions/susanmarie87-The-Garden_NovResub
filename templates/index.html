Who We Are

Gabe's Garden was created when 12-year-old Gabe decided he was fed up with the horrible cafeteria food being served. Together with his family and friends, they have embarked on a mission to change nutrition.

Our Mission

America is home to some of the world's most unhealthy food. Our food supply is mostly processed, full of unhealthy genetically modifed organisms, and is often referred to as frankenfood. Our farms are saturated in chemicals that cause cancer as a form of pest control. As a result, our nation is seeing some of the worst health outcomes in children and adults. Over 40% of America's children suffer from chronic illness and cancer is a leading cause of death.
We are here to change that.
